Common Questions

Quick Answers

Can I own 100% of my business as a foreigner?

Yes. Full foreign ownership is permitted for most commercial and professional activities — both in freezones and on the mainland.

How long does business setup take in Dubai?

A freezone company can be ready in 5 to 10 working days. Mainland setup typically takes 2 to 3 weeks depending on the business activity.

Do I need to be in Dubai to set up?

Not always. Many freezone setups allow full remote registration. Our team guides you through every stage regardless of where you are.

What is included in the setup fee?

Trade license, government registration, and PRO services. Visa fees and office space are quoted separately and outlined clearly upfront.

Which is better — freezone or mainland?

Freezone suits international operations and consultants. Mainland is better if you want to sell directly to UAE clients or work with government.

What documents do I need to get started?

A copy of your passport, current UAE visa if applicable, Emirates ID, and a No Objection Certificate from your sponsor if you are a UAE resident.
